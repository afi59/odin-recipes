Pumpkin Soup Recipe

Image Source: Unsplash (link), Author: Chris Vanhove

Dish Description

Pumpkin soup is a hearty autumn meal you can prepare with ease.

The only appliances you'll need are a stove and an immersion blender.

Ingredients

  • a tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 butternut squash or hokkaido pumpkin
  • 1 onion
  • 1 leek
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 4 medium sized potatoes
  • Spices: nutmeg, ginger and salt (you can add your own)
  • Optional: sour cream

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and wash other ingredients(vegetables).
  2. Cut the vegetables in small cubes.
  3. Cut the onions and garlic into small pieces.
  4. To the stove add a pot with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Let the oil heat up. Then add chopped onion and garlic. Occasionally stir.
  5. When the onion and garlic become a bit brown, add leek and stir occasionally. Cover the pot with a lid and let them simmer.
  6. After 3 to 5 minutes, add the cut up vegetables and spices.
  7. Let the vegetables cook for one hour. If they are sticking to the bottom of the pot, add a bit of water (cca. 100ml at a time).
  8. When the vegetables are fully cooked, let them cool down.
  9. When they are warm (not hot) to the touch, blend them with an immersion blender.
    10. Optional step: Heat up the pumpkin soup afterwards and add a tablespoon of sour cream per serving.
